Introduction

Welcome to the Philippines! The country of the Philippines is best known for its beautiful beaches, rainforests, coral reefs, emerald rice fields and its deep cultures and traditions. The archipelago is home to thousands of islands, 7,641 to be exact. Each island is home to a distinct culture, tradition and landscape, giving Philippines it's vast and deep culture. There are many different reasons why you might choose to live in the Philippines. Standard of living here in the Philippines tends to be a lot more cheaper than it's neighbourig countries. Filipinos are known for their friendliness and hospitality. It is very easy for an English-speaking person to move to the Philippines beceause English is actually their second language, which not many people know about. English is taught in schools and colleges.

There is also a huge variety of landscapes in the Philippines such as the busy, traffic and public transport-filled streets of Manila, the vast, peaceful mountain ranges on the main island of Mindanao and the tourist-filled beaches of Boracay and Palawan. Whether you're a city person or a countryside person, there is certainly a perfect spot for you to stay somewhere in the Philippines. Philippines also has a wide variety of unique foods with tastes you would not find anywhere else in the world, from savory dishes such as our national dish adobo to more sweeter dishes like halo-halo and ube ice cream.
